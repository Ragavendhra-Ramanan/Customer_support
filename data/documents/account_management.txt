Account Management

Create an account to track orders, save payment methods, and get exclusive offers.

To create an account, click Sign Up in the top right corner. Enter your email and create a password. You will receive a confirmation email.

Forgot your password? Click Forgot Password on the login page. Enter your email and we will send a reset link.

Update your account information anytime by logging in and clicking Account Settings. You can change your email, password, shipping address, and payment methods.

Delete your account by contacting customer support. We will process the request within 24 hours. Note that deleted accounts cannot be recovered.
