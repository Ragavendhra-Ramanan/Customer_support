Shipping Information

We offer several shipping options:

Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days, free on orders over $50
Express Shipping: 2-3 business days, $15 flat rate
Overnight Shipping: Next business day, $30 flat rate

Orders placed before 2 PM EST ship the same day. Orders after 2 PM ship the next business day.

We ship to all 50 US states and internationally to over 100 countries. International shipping times vary by destination.

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Track your order anytime through your account dashboard.
